Worlds #1 Lasagna

This receipe will show you, how you can make the best lasagna in the world.

For over 20 years this has been one of the most used receipes on our website

Ingridients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4th pound of lean ground beef
  • half cup minced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese

Cooking Directions

  1. Cook Sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat
  2. Stir in tomates and water, Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning and pepper
  3. Bring large pot of Water to boil, coog lasagna noodles for 8 to 19mins, drain and rinse with cold water
  4. In a bowl combine ricotta, egg parsley and salt
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  6. LASAGNA ASSEMBLE
  7. Bake for 25 mins, remove foil and bake for another 25 mins
